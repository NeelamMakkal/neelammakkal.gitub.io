OUR PURPOSE

Founder Vasanth believes that every citizen of India must read and understand the ideologies of Babasaheb Ambedkar, Thanthai Periyar, Karl Marx, and other revolutionary leaders. This platform is built to reclaim the history that was suppressed or hidden from the people.

"My name is Vasanth. I come from the land where Thanthai Periyar lived—a land where attaching a caste surname is considered a sign of regression. Thanthai Periyar taught us that as long as the surname is not discarded, the hierarchy will never end. This is why I use only my first name. Jai Bhim."
