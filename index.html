OUR PURPOSE

Founder Vasanth believes that every citizen of India must read and understand the ideologies of Babasaheb Ambedkar, Thanthai Periyar, Karl Marx, and other revolutionary leaders. This platform is built to reclaim the history that was suppressed or hidden from the people.

Our mission is to provide these books free of cost so that every single citizen can read and awaken their consciousness. Jai Bhim.

FOUNDER OF NEELAM MAKKAL: VASANTH

The Founder's Story

"My name is Vasanth. You might wonder why I only use my first name. I come from the land where Thanthai Periyar lived—a land where attaching a caste surname is considered a sign of regression. There is no need for it."

"Thanthai Periyar E.V. Ramaswamy taught us that as long as the surname—which holds the seed of caste discrimination—is not discarded, the hierarchy of high and low will never end. This is why he threw away his own caste name. Similarly, Babasaheb Ambedkar chose the path of Buddhism to leave behind the discrimination of the caste system."

"To advance their vision, I started Neelam Makkal. Through this, I aim to amplify their mission. Thank you. Jai Bhim."
