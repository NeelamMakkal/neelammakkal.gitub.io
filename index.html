OUR PURPOSE

Neelam Makkal is founded on the principle that the liberation of mind is the greatest revolution. We believe every citizen of India must have access to the works of Babasaheb Ambedkar. Founder Mr. Vasanth provides this digital library Free of Cost to empower every individual with the weapon of knowledge.
