ABOUT US & FOUNDER

Founder of Neelam Makkal: Vasanth

I know exactly what casteism is because I belong to such a family. The world that looks at casteism is never going to change. It is 2026, and if anyone thinks casteism has ended, just look at your own name. If a surname is attached to your name, you haven't left casteism, friend; you are holding onto it. You proudly say names like Thakur, Sharma, or Bhardwaj, continuing the game of casteism. This is why my mentor Periyar said that as long as there is a surname behind the name, casteism will never end. He changed his name from Ramaswamy Naicker to E.V. Ramaswamy. I also belong to that Tamil Nadu lineage. My name is simply Vasanth.

JAI BHIM
