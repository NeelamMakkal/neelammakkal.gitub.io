OUR PURPOSE & VISION

Founder Vasanth believes that every citizen of India must read and understand the ideologies of Babasaheb Ambedkar, Thanthai Periyar, Karl Marx, and other revolutionary leaders. This platform is not just a digital library; it is a movement to reclaim the history that was suppressed or hidden from the people. We aim to provide free access to books that ignite the spark of social justice and equality in every mind.

MEET THE FOUNDER

VASANTH | FOUNDER

"My name is Vasanth. You might wonder why I only use my first name. I come from the land where Thanthai Periyar lived—a land where attaching a caste surname is considered a sign of regression. Thanthai Periyar E.V. Ramaswamy taught us that as long as the surname—which holds the seed of caste discrimination—is not discarded, the hierarchy of high and low will never end. This is why he threw away his own caste name. Similarly, Babasaheb Ambedkar chose the path of Buddhism to leave behind the discrimination of the caste system. To advance their vision, I started Neelam Makkal. Jai Bhim."
