ABOUT NEELAM MAKKAL

The core purpose of Neelam Makkal is to ensure that every citizen of India can read and understand the works of Babasaheb Ambedkar, Thanthai Periyar, Karl Marx, and many other revolutionary leaders.


Our goal is to reclaim the history that has been suppressed or hidden from the people. We provide these books free of cost so that every individual in this nation can empower themselves through knowledge. This is the ultimate mission of Neelam Makkal. JAI BHIM.

FOUNDER OF NEELAM MAKKAL - VASANTH

A Message from Vasanth

My name is Vasanth. You might wonder why I only share my first name. I come from the land where Thanthai Periyar lived—a land where attaching a surname to one's name is seen as a sign of inequality.

My mentor Thanthai Periyar E.V. Ramaswamy taught me that as long as these surnames, which represent caste and hierarchy, exist, discrimination will never end. This is why he discarded his own surname. Seeing this same struggle, Babasaheb Ambedkar joined Buddhism to escape the caste-based oppression of Hindu society.

To carry their legacy forward, I started Neelam Makkal. Through this, I aim to amplify their goals. Thank you. Jai Bhim.
