OUR PURPOSE

Founder Vasanth believes that every citizen must understand the ideologies of Ambedkar, Periyar, and Karl Marx. This platform is built to reclaim the history that was suppressed. Our mission is to provide these books free of cost to awaken the consciousness of the masses.
