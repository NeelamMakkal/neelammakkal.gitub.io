Our Purpose

Neelam Makkal follows the path of Ambedkar, Periyar, and Karl Marx. Founder Vasanth established this platform so every citizen of India can learn about their true, suppressed history. From Babasaheb’s struggle for the Constitution to Periyar’s revolution in Tamil Nadu and Marx’s vision of a communist world—we bring it all to you for free.

Founder of Neelam Makkal: Vasanth

"I know exactly what casteism is because I belong to such a family. If you think casteism is gone in 2026, look at your name. If you still carry a surname like Thakur, Sharma, or Bhardwaj with pride, you haven't left casteism—you are clutching it. My mentor Periyar said casteism won't end as long as surnames exist. That’s why he changed his name from Ramaswamy Naicker to E.V. Ramaswamy. I belong to that same Tamil soil. My name is simply Vasanth. JAI BHIM."
