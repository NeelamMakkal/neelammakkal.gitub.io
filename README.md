Neelam Makkal - Digital Library 📚
Neelam Makkal is a digital platform dedicated to bringing true history, constitutional literacy, and revolutionary ideologies to every citizen of India. We believe that education and awareness are the primary tools for social transformation.
✊ Our Mission
The core purpose of this website is to provide easy access to the books and histories of great leaders whose contributions and ideas have often been suppressed or sidelined. We aim to empower people to read and understand:
Babasaheb Dr. B.R. Ambedkar: For constitutional rights and social justice.
Periyar E.V. Ramasamy: For rationalism and equality.
Karl Marx: For understanding labor rights and class struggle.
Our Suppressed History: Bringing to light the facts and narratives that have been hidden from the public eye.
